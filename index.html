About Me

Startup Information


Inedit (Brandazine) is a fashion retail service for content creators. It is an exclusive fashion rental service for creators on Instagram and Youtube. Creators pay a monthly subscription to gain unlimited access to fashion products and the opportunity to sell their content to brands. They were founded in 2020 and are based in South Korea with a team size of 10. The company is co-founded by Matt and Keon, who’ve both been in the creator space for over 4 years, as well as Ikhan, whose co-founded a fashion rental company previously. Brandazine has grown to $80K MRR with 100 brands and 500 creators and have stayed profitable for the past 5 months through positive unit economics. The users of this application include content creators and the fashion brands. Based on a conversation with the co-founder, Keon, there seems to be a content creator side of the product for mobile, but no mobile product for the brand side yet. Here is the link to the startup's website.
