Startup Information


Inedit (Brandazine) is a fashion retail service for content creators. It is an exclusive fashion rental service for creators on Instagram and Youtube. Creators pay a monthly subscription to gain unlimited access to fashion products and the opportunity to sell their content to brands. They were founded in 2020 and are based in South Korea with a team size of 10. The users of this application include content creators and the fashion brands.
